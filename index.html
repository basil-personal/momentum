Features

Designed for how you actually work

Every detail considered. No learning curve, no configuration screens, no cognitive overhead.

Hover-hand gesture

Hold your hand over the device to instantly pause recording. A proximity sensor detects the gesture — no buttons, no tapping, no fumbling.

NFC tap to transcript

Tap your phone against the device and your full transcript appears instantly. No app to open, no pairing screen, no loading spinner. Just tap and read.

Always-on ambient listening

The device is always ready. It wakes automatically when conversation begins and captures everything — no "start recording" button to remember.

Works with existing tools

Connects directly to Otter, Granola, Quill, and more. Your transcripts flow into the apps you already use — no proprietary ecosystem to buy into.

Multi-room, multi-device

Deploy across your office — every conference room, every huddle space. Devices sync to a single dashboard and share a unified transcript history.

Privacy-first architecture

Audio is processed on-device and encrypted end-to-end. No raw audio is stored in the cloud. You own your data — always.

Speaker identification

Automatically distinguishes between speakers in a conversation. Transcripts are labeled by participant, making follow-ups and action items clear.

12–18 hour battery

The Personal lasts 12 hours, the Conference 18. Enough for a full day of meetings. USB-C fast charging gets you 4 hours in 15 minutes.

Searchable history

Full-text search across every conversation ever captured. Find that one thing someone said three weeks ago in seconds — no more lost context.

How It Works

Three steps. Zero learning curve.

1

Set it down

Place the device on any surface. No pairing, no setup wizard, no companion app required. The amber LED pulses once — it's ready.

Talk naturally. The device captures everything — multiple speakers, crosstalk, whispers — with broadcast-quality clarity. Hover your hand to pause anytime.

3

Tap to get your transcript

Tap your phone to the device. Your transcript appears instantly via NFC — already synced to your AI notetaking tool with summaries, action items, and highlights.

Security & Privacy

Your conversations are yours

Privacy isn't a feature — it's the architecture. Every design decision starts with the question: how do we capture less, not more?

End-to-end encryption

All audio and transcripts are encrypted with AES-256 in transit and at rest. Only you and your authorized team members can access your data.

On-device processing

Initial audio processing happens on the device itself. Raw audio never leaves the hardware — only encrypted, compressed transcription data is transmitted.

Compliance ready

SOC 2 Type II certified. HIPAA-eligible configuration available. GDPR-compliant data handling with configurable retention and deletion policies.
