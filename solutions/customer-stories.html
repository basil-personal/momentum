Customer Stories

How teams use Momentum

From two-person startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, teams across every industry are using Momentum to capture conversations, transcribe them with built-in AI, and focus on the work that matters.

Enterprise

Scaling knowledge capture across 200+ meeting rooms

A global consulting firm deployed Momentum Conference devices across every office. With built-in transcription and automatic syncing to their internal knowledge base, consultants no longer spend hours writing meeting recaps. Action items are extracted instantly and pushed to their project management tools.

"We estimated it saves each consultant 5 hours per week. Across the firm, that's transformational."
Healthcare

Giving clinicians their time back

A regional hospital network uses Momentum Personal devices so physicians can focus on patients instead of typing notes. Conversations are transcribed on-device with HIPAA-compliant processing, and structured summaries flow directly into their EHR system. Doctors report spending 40% less time on documentation.

"I can actually look my patients in the eye again. The device just sits there quietly and handles the rest."
Education

Making every lecture accessible

A university's disability services office placed Momentum Conference devices in 50 lecture halls. Students with hearing impairments or learning differences receive real-time transcripts on their phones via NFC tap. Professors use the AI-generated summaries to create better study guides. The devices replaced a fragmented system of volunteer note-takers and expensive captioning services.

"Every student gets the same high-quality notes now, regardless of ability. It's an equity win."
Legal

Capturing depositions and client meetings with precision

A mid-size law firm uses Momentum devices for client intake meetings, depositions, and internal strategy sessions. The built-in transcription captures speaker-attributed dialogue with timestamps, and attorneys can search across their entire conversation history. They also plug into Otter.ai for matters where their clients prefer a specific platform.

"The accuracy is remarkable. We've reduced our transcription vendor costs by 80%."
Startups

Moving fast without losing context

A 12-person seed-stage startup uses Momentum Personal devices for every meeting — standups, investor calls, customer interviews. With Momentum's built-in AI, they get summaries and action items without paying for another SaaS tool. When their board members prefer Granola, transcripts sync there too. Everyone stays aligned without anyone taking notes.

"We killed our meeting notes Slack channel. Everything just shows up where it needs to be."
Remote Teams

Bridging the async gap

A distributed product team across four time zones uses Momentum Conference devices in their two office hubs and Personal devices for remote workers. Transcripts with AI summaries are automatically posted to Slack channels after every meeting, so team members in other time zones wake up to complete context — not a vague "we discussed the roadmap" message.

"Our async culture actually works now. No one misses context just because they're in a different time zone."
