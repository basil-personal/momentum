Getting Started

How do I set up my Momentum device for the first time?

Download the Momentum app from the App Store or Google Play. Create an account, then hold your phone near the device to pair via NFC. The app will walk you through a short calibration process and your first test recording. The entire setup takes about three minutes.

What phones are compatible with Momentum?

Momentum works with any iPhone running iOS 16 or later and any Android device running Android 13 or later with NFC support. Most flagship and mid-range phones released since 2022 are fully compatible.

Do I need a subscription to use Momentum?

The device and app work without a subscription for on-device recording and local transcription. A Momentum Pro subscription ($9.99/month) unlocks cloud sync, AI-powered search, smart highlights, and integrations with third-party tools.

How long does the battery last?

Momentum Personal lasts up to 12 hours of continuous recording on a single charge. Momentum Conference lasts up to 18 hours. Both devices support USB-C fast charging, reaching 80% in approximately 25 minutes.

Can I use Momentum without the app?

Yes. The device records independently and stores transcripts in its on-device storage (32 GB for Personal, 64 GB for Conference). However, the app is needed for search, highlights, device management, and syncing transcripts to the cloud.

Device & Hardware

Is Momentum waterproof?

Momentum Personal is rated IPX4, which means it is protected against splashes from any direction. It can handle rain or sweat but should not be submerged in water. Momentum Conference is designed for indoor use and is not water-resistant.

How do I reset my device?

Press and hold the button on the underside of the device for 10 seconds until the amber LED flashes rapidly. This performs a soft reset. For a factory reset, go to Settings → Device → Factory Reset in the Momentum app. Note that a factory reset erases all on-device recordings.

Can I replace the magnetic clip?

Yes. Replacement magnetic clips are available in our accessories store for $24. The clip uses a standard attachment mechanism and can be swapped in seconds without any tools.

How do firmware updates work?

Firmware updates are delivered over-the-air through the Momentum app. When an update is available, you will see a notification in the app. Updates typically take 2–5 minutes and the device will restart automatically when finished.

What is the recording range?

Momentum Personal is optimised for conversations within 1–2 metres, ideal for one-on-one meetings and personal use. Momentum Conference captures audio from up to 8 metres away in a full 360-degree radius.

Transcription & AI

How accurate is the transcription?

Momentum achieves over 95% accuracy in quiet environments and over 90% in noisy settings. Accuracy improves over time as the system learns your vocabulary and the voices of frequent speakers.

What languages are supported?

Momentum currently supports English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, and Mandarin Chinese. Additional languages are added regularly through firmware and cloud updates.

How does speaker identification work?

Momentum creates voice profiles for each speaker it encounters. After a few conversations, it can reliably distinguish between speakers and label them in transcripts. You can assign names to voice profiles in the app for automatic labelling.

Can I search across all my transcripts at once?

Yes. The AI-powered search feature lets you ask natural language questions across your entire transcript history. For example, “When did we decide on the launch date?” will find the relevant moment regardless of which recording it came from.

Account & Billing

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → Subscription in the Momentum app or on the web dashboard. Click “Cancel Subscription” and follow the prompts. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees apply.

Can I transfer my device to another person?

Yes. Perform a factory reset on the device, which removes all data and account associations. The new owner can then pair it with their own Momentum account as if it were new.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

If you cancel your Pro subscription, your transcripts remain accessible in read-only mode for 90 days. After that, cloud-stored transcripts are permanently deleted. Recordings stored on-device are not affected by subscription changes.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all hardware purchases. If you are not happy with your Momentum device, contact support within 30 days for a full refund. Subscription refunds are prorated for the unused portion of the billing period.
