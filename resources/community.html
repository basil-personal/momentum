Community Guidelines

We want the Momentum community to be a welcoming, productive space for everyone. Here is what we ask of all members:

  • Be respectful. Treat everyone with kindness, regardless of experience level or background. Disagreements are fine; personal attacks are not.
  • Be helpful. If someone asks a question you know the answer to, take a moment to share. The best communities are built on generosity.
  • Stay on topic. Keep conversations relevant to Momentum, productivity, recording technology, and related subjects.
  • Protect privacy. Never share recordings, transcripts, or personal information belonging to others without their explicit consent.
  • Report problems. If you see behaviour that violates these guidelines, report it to the moderation team. We take every report seriously.

Featured Community Projects

🚀

momentum-obsidian

A community-built plugin that syncs Momentum transcripts and highlights directly into Obsidian vaults. Supports daily notes, tags, and backlinks. Built by @danielkvist with over 800 installs.

📊

meeting-analytics

An open-source dashboard that visualises your meeting patterns: talk-time ratios, meeting frequency, topic trends, and more. Uses the Momentum API and ships as a self-hosted Docker container. Built by @priyasharma.

🤖

momentum-bot

A Slack bot that posts transcript summaries and action items to a channel after each meeting. Configurable per-channel with keyword filters and mention alerts. Built by @marcuslee and used by 60+ teams.
