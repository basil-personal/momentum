The Momentum App

Your conversations, searchable and organised. The companion app turns raw recordings into a personal knowledge base you can query in natural language.

Download

Your Second Brain, in Your Pocket

The Momentum app is where your recordings become useful. Every conversation is transcribed, indexed, and made searchable. Ask questions in plain English and get precise answers drawn from your entire history. No more scrolling through notes — just ask.

Features

📡

NFC Tap-to-Connect

Hold your phone near any Momentum device and the app connects instantly. No Bluetooth menu, no pairing codes, no friction. Tap and go.

🔎

Natural Language Search

Type questions like “What was the deadline Alex mentioned on Tuesday?” and get the exact moment from your transcript, with full context around it.

⭐

Smart Highlights

Momentum automatically surfaces action items, decisions, follow-ups, and key moments. Review a 60-minute meeting in 90 seconds by scanning the highlights.

📱

Device Management

Check battery levels, update firmware, configure recording preferences, and manage multiple devices from a single screen.

🔌

Offline Mode

Access your recent transcripts and highlights even without an internet connection. Searches sync automatically when you reconnect.

🔒

Privacy Controls

Delete recordings, manage retention periods, control what syncs to the cloud, and lock the app with biometric authentication.

How It Works

Step 1
Tap to pair
Hold your phone near your Momentum device. The app detects it via NFC and pairs instantly.
Step 2
Record naturally
Go about your day. Momentum records in the background, processing audio on-device in real time.
Step 3
Sync and search
Transcripts sync to the app when you are ready. Search, review highlights, or share excerpts with your team.
