Momentum is defining the ambient computing category. Here is why we believe this is a generational opportunity.

Investment Highlights

Massive Market

The global productivity software market is valued at $82 billion and growing at 14% annually. The wearable technology segment is projected to reach $230 billion by 2032. Momentum sits at the intersection of both — a new category we call ambient productivity, where hardware and AI merge to eliminate the overhead of knowledge work.

Strong Traction

Since shipping our first hardware in Q3 2025, Momentum has onboarded over 12,000 active users across 400 companies. Net revenue retention stands at 145%, driven by organic team expansion within accounts. Our waitlist currently holds 38,000 names, and customer NPS is 74 — placing us in the top decile of hardware companies.

World-Class Team

Our 45-person team includes alumni from Apple, Google DeepMind, Sonos, Linear, and Teenage Engineering. The founding team has shipped hardware products used by tens of millions of people. We combine deep expertise in embedded systems, on-device ML, and consumer product design — a rare combination that is extremely difficult to replicate.

Funding History

June 2024

Seed Round — $6M

Led by Abstract Ventures with participation from Forty Seven Fund and a syndicate of angel investors including former VP of Hardware at Apple and the co-founder of Notion. Funds used to build the initial prototype, hire the founding engineering team, and conduct early user testing.

September 2025

Series A — $22M

Led by Sequoia Capital with Abstract Ventures following on. Funding is being deployed toward manufacturing scale-up, expanding the engineering and go-to-market teams, and building out the enterprise product tier. The round was significantly oversubscribed.

2026 (Planned)

Series B

We anticipate raising a growth round in the second half of 2026 to fund international expansion, next-generation hardware development, and scaling our enterprise sales organization.
