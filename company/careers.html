How We Work

We have strong opinions about what makes a team thrive. Here are the principles that shape our days.

Async-First

Meetings are a last resort, not a default. We write proposals, record Loom walkthroughs, and make decisions in documents. Most of our team overlaps only four hours a day — and that is by design. Deep work needs uninterrupted time, and we protect it fiercely.

Craft Over Speed

We ship often, but we never ship sloppy. Every pull request gets a thoughtful review. Every interface gets sweated over. We would rather delay a release by a week than push something that feels 90% there. The last ten percent is where the magic lives.

Small Teams, Big Trust

We organize around two-pizza squads with full autonomy over their domain. No permission-seeking, no approval chains. You own your roadmap, your architecture, and your outcomes. Leadership's job is to provide context and clear obstacles — nothing more.

Benefits & Perks

Health, Dental & Vision

Comprehensive medical coverage for you and your dependents. We cover 100% of employee premiums and 75% of dependent premiums through Anthem Blue Cross.

Unlimited PTO

We trust you to manage your time. Take what you need, when you need it. We also enforce a minimum of three weeks off per year because rest is productive.

Equipment Budget

$5,000 to set up your workspace exactly the way you want it — laptop, monitor, keyboard, chair, standing desk. Refreshed every two years.

Learning Stipend

$2,000 per year for conferences, courses, books, or coaching. We believe growth is part of the job, not something you do on your own time.

Home Office Setup

For remote employees, we provide a one-time $3,000 stipend for furniture and ergonomics, plus $150 per month for internet and coworking memberships.

Annual Retreat

Once a year the entire company gathers for a week-long offsite. Past locations include Big Sur, Tulum, and Lisbon. It is part strategy, part bonding, all memorable.
