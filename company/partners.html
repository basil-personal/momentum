Partner Program

Build with us. Ambient computing is bigger than any one company — let's shape the ecosystem together.

Why Partner with Momentum

Momentum is building the ambient layer between people and their work. But ambient computing only reaches its potential when it connects seamlessly with the tools professionals already rely on — calendars, CRMs, project management platforms, communication suites, and more.

Our partner program is designed for companies that share our belief in calm, privacy-respecting technology. By partnering with Momentum, you gain early access to our device APIs, co-marketing opportunities, and a direct line to our engineering and product teams. Together, we can create integrations that feel native and effortless for end users.

We are intentionally selective. We partner deeply with a small number of companies rather than broadly with hundreds. Every partnership gets dedicated engineering support, joint go-to-market planning, and a shared commitment to quality.

Partnership Tracks

Technology Partners

For SaaS platforms and productivity tools that want to integrate directly with the Momentum device ecosystem. Technology partners receive full API access, sandbox hardware for development, and co-engineering support to build native integrations. Current technology partners include calendar, project management, and CRM platforms.

Channel Partners

For resellers, system integrators, and managed service providers who want to bring Momentum to their enterprise customers. Channel partners receive volume pricing, sales enablement materials, demo hardware, and dedicated account management. We support partners through the entire sales cycle from discovery to deployment.

Integration Partners

For developers and agencies building custom workflows on top of the Momentum platform. Integration partners get access to our webhook system, transcript APIs, and action-item pipelines. Build automations that route meeting insights into any downstream system your clients use.

What You Get

Early API Access

Partners receive access to pre-release APIs and beta firmware builds, giving you a head start on building integrations before public launch.

Sandbox Hardware

We ship development-grade Momentum Pins to every technology and integration partner at no cost, including replacement units as hardware evolves.

Co-Marketing

Joint blog posts, case studies, webinars, and inclusion in our partner directory — which is prominently featured on our website and in our onboarding flow.

Dedicated Support

A named partner engineer and partner manager for your account. Slack Connect channel, priority bug triage, and quarterly business reviews.

Revenue Sharing

Channel partners earn competitive commissions on hardware and subscription revenue. Integration partners can monetize custom workflows through our marketplace.

Partner Advisory Board

Top partners are invited to our quarterly advisory sessions where we share roadmap previews and gather feedback that directly shapes product decisions.
