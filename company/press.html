Company Overview

Momentum is an ambient computing company headquartered in New York City. Founded in 2024 by Elara Voss and Marcus Chen, the company builds wearable hardware and on-device AI that captures, organizes, and surfaces meeting context — without requiring any manual input from the user.

The company's debut product, the Momentum Pin, is a lightweight wearable that listens to in-person and virtual conversations, generates real-time summaries, extracts action items, and syncs everything to the user's existing workflow tools. All audio processing happens on-device, and no recordings are stored in the cloud.

Momentum has raised $28 million in funding from Sequoia Capital, Abstract Ventures, and a group of angel investors including former executives from Apple, Notion, and Figma. The company employs 45 people across its New York City headquarters and a distributed remote team.
